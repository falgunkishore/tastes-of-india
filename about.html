About

Tastes of India is a simple recipe site focused on home-style Indian cooking. The goal is to make traditional dishes easier to understand and cook.

Indian food can feel complicated, but it doesn’t have to be. This site explains recipes in a clear way so you know what you're doing and why.

What You'll Find

  • Easy-to-follow Indian recipes
  • Common ingredients and simple methods
  • Dishes from different parts of India

About the Author

Created by a home cook who enjoys making and sharing Indian food. Every recipe is tested in a regular kitchen and meant to be practical.
